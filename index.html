Vision in Action

Manifesto Rules

  • Zero corruption governance: transparent digital tenders, asset disclosure, and time-bound anti-graft trials.
  • Exam justice law: national anti-paper-leak authority, encrypted logistics, and strict criminal action against exam mafias.
  • No shelter for sexual crimes: special fast-track courts and lifetime disqualification from public office on conviction.
  • Constitutional accountability: remove criminals from the system through due process, independent investigation, and court-monitored action.
  • Clean air and water rights: enforceable pollution limits, river restoration missions, and penalties for violators.
  • Anti-defection integrity: leaders who switch parties after election should face immediate seat resignation and public re-mandate.
  • Public service pledge: every elected representative must publish yearly performance and constituency progress reports.

Ending Key Problems of India

Corruption

End cash-based leakages through digital governance, social audits, whistleblower protection, and independent anti-corruption prosecution.

Paper Leak Cases

Use secure exam technology, audit trails, and lifetime bans plus jail terms for organized paper-leak networks.

Rapists in the System

Mandatory suspension during trial in sensitive roles, survivor protection units, and strict legal removal from authority after conviction.

Air & Water Crisis

City-level clean air targets, industrial accountability, groundwater protection, and safe drinking water in every household.

Party-Switching Leaders

Strong anti-defection reforms so elected leaders cannot betray the voter mandate without going back to the people.
