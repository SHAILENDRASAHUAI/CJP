A Bold Public Movement for Honest, Safe, and Clean Governance

Cockroach Janata Party is built as a citizen-led platform focused on accountability, equal opportunity, women’s safety, and environmental dignity. The mission is simple: public power must return to the public.

Vision: Janata Ka Sankalp

Build an India where public systems reward honesty, youth get fair chances, women feel secure, and local communities drive decisions through transparent democratic participation.

100% Transparent Tenders Digital procurement with public audit trails.
Fast Justice Focus Time-bound legal support for serious civic crimes.
Youth Opportunity Merit-first systems and anti-paper-leak enforcement.
Clean India Mandate Air and water restoration with measurable targets.

10-Point National Agenda

A policy-first framework focused on governance reform and citizen dignity.

Zero-Corruption Governance

Open budgets, monitored contracts, and strict anti-graft prosecution.

Exam Integrity Law

Encrypted logistics, criminal accountability, and student protection.

Women’s Safety Command

Rapid support desks, legal aid, and neighborhood protection networks.

Public Health Equity

Ward-level clinics, medicine transparency, and stronger preventive care.

Employment Push

Skill-linked local jobs, startup support, and fair recruitment systems.

Constitutional Accountability

Institution-first governance and independent due-process safeguards.

Environmental Rights

Citizen pollution dashboards and strict action against repeat violators.

Rural Infrastructure

Roads, irrigation, and digital connectivity for village prosperity.

Education Modernization

School quality tracking, teacher support, and practical skill pathways.

People’s Report Cards

Annual public performance scorecards for elected representatives.

Movement on Ground

Community work designed to turn local participation into measurable impact.

Citizen Action Units

How Local Chapters Work

Every chapter publishes monthly goals, open meeting notes, and a local progress dashboard. Residents can vote on top priorities and track follow-up actions.

Roadmap to National Impact

A phased timeline for building a trusted and transparent grassroots platform.

1

Build verified local volunteer teams

Focus on ward-level engagement, data collection, and issue mapping.

2

Launch district policy forums

Turn local concerns into public charters with measurable commitments.

3

Publish quarterly public accountability reports

Share progress transparently and invite citizens to validate outcomes.

4

Scale community-led reform campaigns nationally

Replicate successful local models and sustain long-term governance pressure.
